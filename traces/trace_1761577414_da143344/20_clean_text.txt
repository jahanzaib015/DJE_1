
    This is a sample policy document about investment guidelines.
    
    Section 1: Investment Objectives
    The fund shall invest primarily in equity securities of companies with strong fundamentals.
    The fund may not invest in speculative instruments or high-risk derivatives.
    
    Section 2: Risk Management
    All investments must comply with regulatory requirements.
    The fund is prohibited from investing in companies with poor ESG ratings.
    
    Section 3: Reporting Requirements
    Monthly reports must be submitted to the board.
    Quarterly performance reviews are mandatory.
